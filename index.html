Privacy

Apna Khata

Short summary: Apna Khata stores only the notes you create — entirely on your device. The app works completely offline, does not use the internet, and requires no permissions.

What we collect

No personal data, analytics, or usage data is collected or transmitted.

Where notes are stored

All notes are saved locally on your device. They are not synced, uploaded, or backed up by the app.

Permissions

The app is designed to run without requesting Android runtime permissions. If your device prompts for a permission, you may deny it and the app will continue to function for basic note storage and viewing.

Third-party services

Apna Khata does not include analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs that send data off-device.
